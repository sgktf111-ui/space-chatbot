About NASA

NASA, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration, was founded in 1958. It leads humanity's exploration of space, from the Moon landings to Mars rovers. Learn more at nasa.gov.

Key Missions

Artemis Program

NASA's plan to return humans to the Moon by 2025 and establish a sustainable presence.

Perseverance Rover

Exploring Mars since 2021, collecting samples for future return to Earth.

Hubble Space Telescope

Launched in 1990, it has captured stunning images of distant galaxies.
